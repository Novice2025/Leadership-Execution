Executive Summary | The Core Principle

The core principle of execution is **clear ownership and process optimization**. Leaders must clearly state who is responsible (e.g., **Joe will lead this project**), focus on efficiency (making things **run smoothly**), and ensure that all issues lead to constructive analysis (**We must investigate the cause of this delay**) rather than blame.

Define the leader, define the process, and define the root cause.
